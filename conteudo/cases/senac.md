# Senac, Digital transformation of an enrollment process

> **An online enrollment platform that eased in-person overcrowding and brought consistency to student registration across Brazil's 28 states.**

## Overview

Senac is one of Brazil's largest education institutions, enrolling students nationwide. Its enrollment depended on in-person offices, which meant overcrowding, long waits, and a process that varied from state to state.

**The challenge.** Make it easy for students to find and enroll in courses online, while standardizing a process that had no single, consistent form across the country.

## My role

As **Lead Designer**, I owned research, design direction, and the design system, from exploratory field research and interviews through high-fidelity prototyping, moderated testing, and iteration.

## Process

### Research
- **Shadowing.** Exploratory research observing the enrollment process firsthand and identifying the pain points of Senac's secretaries.
- **User interviews.** Conversations with students to understand their real experience of enrolling.

### Key findings
1. **Time constraints.** Senac's office hours clashed with students' work schedules, making enrollment hard.
2. **Technology limitations.** Many students had no computer at home and had to travel to a Senac office to enroll.
3. **No process consistency.** Standardizing registration across Brazil's 28 states, each with different requirements, was the central challenge.

### Design decisions
The redesign tackled the technical and usability issues through focused design and content strategy. Direction came from extensive ideation, leaning on Senac's existing intelligence technologies to deliver smart, scalable solutions. I prototyped in Figma, building a design library grounded in Senac's brand guidelines so the platform felt native to the institution.

### Testing & iteration
Moderated testing with 10 students across two rounds surfaced clear usability improvements. Iteration focused on simplifying the web app's copy to remove jargon and resolving the remaining friction points.

## Outcome & impact

Senac launched a marketing and communication campaign to introduce students to the new online enrollment option. Because no prior platform existed, there was no baseline to compare against, but the in-person offices saw noticeably smaller crowds. The platform eased administrative work immediately and was adopted quickly, proving it both simplifies enrollment and widens access for Brazil's adult learners.
