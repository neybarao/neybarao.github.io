## Approach [seção nova, sugestão]

Short section reinforcing how you work. Three principles:

1. **Start with evidence.** Research, analytics, and usability data before pixels, so design solves the real problem.
2. **Design the decision, not the screen.** Every interface choice traces back to a user need or a business goal.
3. **Sweat the details.** Clarity, consistency, and craft are what make a product feel trustworthy.
